Astro is a web framework built around the idea that most websites are content sites — and content sites should ship as little JavaScript as possible. Here's the elevator pitch:

## Islands architecture

Astro renders your pages as static HTML at build time. Interactive components (called "islands") are isolated and can use any framework — React, Vue, Svelte, Solid, or just vanilla web components.

## Content collections

Astro's Content Collections API gives you typed, validated frontmatter for your markdown and MDX files. Combined with TypeScript, you get autocomplete and compile-time safety for your content.

## Built-in optimization

Image optimization, asset bundling, and CSS scoping are all built in. You don't need to wire up Webpack plugins or webpack at all — Astro handles it.

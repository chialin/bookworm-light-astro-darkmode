This theme is a community fork of [Bookworm Light Astro](https://github.com/themefisher/bookworm-light-astro) by Themefisher, a beautifully crafted free Astro blog template. The original is excellent — please consider supporting Themefisher if you find their work valuable.

## Why this fork exists

This fork adds a few things that weren't in the upstream at the time of creation:

- **Dark mode** with View Transitions persistence and dynamic logo switching
- **CSS variable mapping fixes** for proper color theming
- **Aspect ratio constraints** on hero images to prevent layout jumps
- **Author page and similar posts** rendering fixes
- **Typography refinements** that work for both Latin and CJK content

This is an **unofficial fork**, not endorsed by, affiliated with, or sponsored by Themefisher. All credit for the original design and architecture goes to the upstream maintainers.
